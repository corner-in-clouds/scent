You might wondering, what does 'secrets about scents' even mean? Well I'm not here to reveal business secrets like their marketing strategies or their fragrance recipe, the secret here refers to my some of the experiences I've learned about using specific perfumes for specific occasions.

In other words, I'll be introducing some of my perfumes which I use in different occations that requires me to appear with different personalities, if you are interested, follow me and keep reading ♡


The first scenario requires that I appear more approachable and friendly. It frequently happens when I meet strangers or friends who aren't particularly close to me.

The second scenario pertains to more relaxed situations in which I may maintain the atmosphere casual while also wearing perfume that I enjoy, and you guessed it, this scenario occurs when I am with my close friends.

Finally, I am in my final year of university, therefore I need to prepare some perfume that will make me appear more professional and official; they are not necessarily my favourite because they are typically quite powerful.
